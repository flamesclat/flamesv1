ABOUT US

Established in 2019, FLAMES is an initiative of law students to help build up the aptitude of its students towards achieving quality legal education. It firmly believes in the dissemination of quality education especially in the field of law entrances so as to augment the process of integration of legal education in India. FLAMES, since its inception, has given exceptional results with whopping 18+ and 22+ selections in NLUs from its super 40 batch in 2020 and 2021 respectively. We have reposed our unwavering faith in the significance of quality education, and constantly endeavour to fulfil the vacuum of the same, specifically in the field of law. Law as a career can open floodgates of knowledge and enlightenment and can be your gateway to intellectual world and we, here at FLAMES, envision your dreams as our own and help you build the road towards your dream institution of law!
Our Vision at FLAMES- We, at FLAMES, endeavour to spur the process of inclusion of the peripheral groups of the society and help them eject themselves out of the vagaries of haplessness towards the heights of legal erudition to further proliferate this constant process of societal integration .
